The Mandalorian: Sands of Arvala

The Mandalorian: Sands of Arvala is a roguelite game based in the universe of The Mandalorian developed for PC in which you will play as Din Djarin "Mando" chasing the target that you have been tasked to find. All this while you go across multiple levels found in Arvala-7 defeating the troops of the Galactic Empire, dangerous fauna native to the planet and other bounty hunters that are also behind the same target as you.


In The Mandalorian: Sands of Arvala, you will be able to explore dangerous parts of Arvala-7 fighting against different types of enemies in a fast-paced style of combat using multiple guns and tools at your disposal, obtaining upgrades through the run that will enhance the performance of your guns and getting better equippment for the following runs.
