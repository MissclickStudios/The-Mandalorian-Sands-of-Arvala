The Mandalorian: Sands of Arvala

The Mandalorian: Sands of Arvala is a fast-paced roguelite game based in the universe of The Mandalorian developed for PC in which you will play as Din Djarin "Mando". While traversing the planet of Arvala-7 looking for the target of the bounty you have accepted, you will have to defeat troopers of the empire and several fauna native to the planet.
