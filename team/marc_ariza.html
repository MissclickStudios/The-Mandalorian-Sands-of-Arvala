Hello, I am Marc Ariza and I have been the lead designer for The Mandalorian - Sands of Arvala. The main challenge of this project has been to manage a group of designers working together in a fun and interesting design for the game together with everything that this implies. At the same time, I also had to maintain a good communication with the other departments in the project to have an overall positive development and final result.
