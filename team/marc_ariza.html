MARC ARIZA - LEAD DESIGNER

  • Linkedin
  • GitHub

Hello, I am Marc Ariza and I have been the lead designer for The Mandalorian - Sands of Arvala. The main challenge of this project has been to manage a group of designers working together in a fun and interesting design for the game together with everything that this implies. At the same time, I also had to maintain good communication with the other departments in the project to have an overall positive development and final result.

My main task in this project has been to manage and organize all aspects of the design from initial designs up to quality assurance for the final release. Having said this, I have also been in charge of the design of multiple parts of the game throughout the entire development.


PROJECT CONTIBUTION

Management
As a lead, one of my main tasks throughout the entire development was the management of the entire design team, leading the development and organizing the entire design pipeline for each of the different scrum teams.

Design Choices
At the same time I also was in charge of making the final decisions related to the design of the game and when it was required, to cut planned content out of the game to be able to achieve the deadlines.

Game Pilars
Ensuring that the main Game Pillars that we decided the game to be based in are followed as much as possible (Replayability, Permadeath, Fast Combat, Randomness Factor).

Character Design
Developing the initial design concept of the playable characters and their basic characteristics. Developing the final approach to the single playable character in the game and all its elements.

Guns & Items
Designing and developing the progression system together with all its components (multiple guns, items, tiers and abilities & mechanics). I was also in charge of designing and start developing the much more complex initial aproach to the progression system which was discarded later on due to time constraints.

Enemies
Designing the initial approach to the enemy types together with quality assurance in the later stages of the development. Designing the 2nd boss of the game from its initial stages to the final release.

Environment
One of my main tasks throughout the development of the game has been to design the environment. I developed the concepts, ideas and mechanics of the initial environments. I also designed some of the initial blockouts for both levels and all their re-adaptations to each of the rooms, remaking both levels and giving them a better look overall and adding all completed models to each of the blockouts. I was also in charge of placing the game elements in the rooms, together with all decoration present in each of them.

Playtesting
Iterating and testing the different parts of the game making sure they are released in the best posible way.
