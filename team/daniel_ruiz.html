Hello everyone! I am Daniel Ruiz and I have been part of the design team in "The mandalorian, Sands of Arvala". I have always loved to design characters and enemies' stats and behaviours and this was my main task to develop in this project.

This project has helped me a lot to learn specially to communicate between members.

- I have learnt how to communicate between my partners.
- I have learnt how to manage a tasks being scrum master.


PROJECT CONTIBUTION

Enemies design
I have been designing and prototyping the enemies (Sandstorm trooper, Blurrg, Turret, Dark trooper, Sarlacc) behaviour, stats and attacks to make the general idea to the artists and programmers.

Sidekick design
I have been designing and prototyping the ability of Grogu.

Environments
In environments I have been creating some blockouts from the assets that artists made and also I have implemented them in the engine adding the colliders and enemies.

Enemies iterations
I have been balancing stats in order to make a better experience to the player.

Audio design document
This was my smallest contribution in the project, was just making a list for the audio requirements.

Playtesting
I have been playtesting the game to find some bugs and errors which can make the player having such a bad game experience.
