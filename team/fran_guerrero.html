I have been part of the art team, specifically in the 3D department. Most of the project I have been in charge of modeling characters and texture them. Thanks of having more experience in modeling and texturing with software that I learned in an external course, I could manage to produce a lot of models in short times for the reason that I had to combine work with studies. In the middle of the project, I introduce to my mates new software for texturing (Substance painter) and I taught them how to use it. That made that the texturing times and the results improved a lot. I would like to expend more time in this project but I enjoyed a lot the time that I've invest and I am excited to know that one day I will work in this extraordinary sector.
