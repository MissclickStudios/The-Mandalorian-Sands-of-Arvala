Hi, My name is Aitor Luque and I have been working as a graphics and gameplay programmer in the development of The Mandalorian: Sands of Arvala. The development of this project has brought me a new perspective and motivations about game development, I enjoyed every task and feature that I had to develop altough I found myself mostly passionate about Graphics programming.
Working with my 23 other teammates was as challenging as rewarding, getting to work side by side and learn from excellent programmers was a great experience.
